Warm-up

Dynamic warm-up and arm care so we start on time. On the whistle at 10:00 sharp, we play.

10:00

Gopher 3s

Groups of three between the 10-foot line and the net. Tilt passing both directions, everyone touching balls at once. Final rounds are scored, and the winning group picks first in the next drill.

10:15

Directional Passing

Full-court passing through all six positions. Pass to the target, then finish in coverage, every single ball. Talk on everything.

10:30

Up/Downs

Four teams, two courts. Three-minute games: win and move toward the top court, lose and defend the bottom one. The team that holds the top court the longest wins the morning.
