Warm-up

Dynamic warm-up and arm care so we start on time. On the whistle at 10:00 sharp, we play.

10:00

Gopher 3s

Triangles of three, about 10 feet apart: two tossers, one passer moving between them, tilt-passing every ball right back at the top of the tosser's head. Everyone rotates through all three spots.

10:15

Directional Passing

Ball control on the move, in every direction. Tossers, passers, and targets rotate lines continuously; passers work from zones 4, 3, and 2, passing into zones 5, 6, and 1, and finish every rep in coverage.

10:30

Up/Downs

Four teams, two courts. Three-minute games: win and move toward the top court, lose and defend the bottom one. The team that holds the top court the longest wins the morning.
