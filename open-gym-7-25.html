Warm-up

Dynamic warm-up and arm care so we start on time. On the whistle at 4:00 sharp, we play.

4:00

Speed Close

Ball control with urgency. The toss comes the instant you touch the dot, so you sprint, close to the ball, drop the inside shoulder, and drag the pass back to target.

4:10

Straight Line Tilt

Rotational passing down a straight line: quiet platform, no swinging arms. You redirect every ball to a target off to the side by dropping the inside shoulder, the tilt we started building on 7/11.

4:20

Partner Weave

Pairs work one ball the full length of the court, passing on the move and leading each other to space. Talking is part of the drill: silent pairs stall, loud pairs fly.

4:30

Serve Receive Progression

Half the gym serves, half passes, then we flip. We build through the four steps: pick your side early, read the serve's direction and distance, make your angle with your feet, and hold the platform to target.

5:00

6 on 6
